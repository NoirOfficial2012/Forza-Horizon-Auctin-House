How To play:
First We have to enter our user name then we have to choose a car brand and a car then we start the auction and we have to buy the cars before others.then we have to sell them for a profit.
The whole Point of the game is to buy out cars before the ai (it can buy them in 0.1-3secs)
And pls pls pls put the correct brand and the correct car together (the game does not recognise wrong cars mixed with wrong brands)
Pls note that the rarer cars take longer to show up and if they still do not show up after a bit of time ur budget is to low for that car.
